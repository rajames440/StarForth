\section*{Corrective Actions: December 2025 Audit (Historical)}

Following the December 2025 codebase audit, 21 of 22 identified issues were
resolved across StarForth v3.0.1. The corrective actions addressed the critical
findings---duplicate profiler definitions, \texttt{LOG\_LINE\_MAX} constant
conflict, and duplicate documentation---as well as the full backlog of
medium-priority items. Each resolution was recorded with its associated commit
hash, providing a complete audit trail from finding to fix. One minor issue was
deferred to a subsequent release. This record is retained as the closure
document for the December 2025 quality remediation program.
