\section*{Pre-Submission Paper Draft (Superseded)}

This 18-page Markdown draft was the working manuscript submitted for peer
review, comprising abstract, eight numbered sections, four formal theorems with
machine-checkable proofs, and appendices reproducing experimental data tables.
The draft established the experimental methodology (90-run randomized trial,
three configurations), the variance decomposition finding (0\% algorithmic,
70\% OS-scheduling), and the adaptive convergence result ($-25.4\%$ under
C\_FULL). The document is superseded by the published \textsc{ssrn} paper
\textit{James: Steady-State Convergence in an Adaptive Runtime}. Researchers
should cite the \textsc{ssrn} version, not this draft.
