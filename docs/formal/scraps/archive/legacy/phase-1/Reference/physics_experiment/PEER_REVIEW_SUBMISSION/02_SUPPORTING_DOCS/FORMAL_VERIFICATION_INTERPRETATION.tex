\section*{Formal Verification Interpretation of Variance Analysis (Superseded)}

This supporting document reframed the physics engine variance analysis through
the lens of formal verification, arguing that the decomposition of execution-time
variance into an algorithmic component (0\% coefficient of variation, cache
configuration) and an OS-scheduling component (60--70\% coefficient of
variation) constitutes evidence of a formally verifiable determinism property.
The key metrics---0\% algorithmic variance, 70\% OS variance, and $-25.4\%$
convergence under C\_FULL---are reported in the published \textsc{ssrn} paper.
This interpretation document is superseded by that publication and by the
Isabelle/HOL theory files in \texttt{proof/}.
