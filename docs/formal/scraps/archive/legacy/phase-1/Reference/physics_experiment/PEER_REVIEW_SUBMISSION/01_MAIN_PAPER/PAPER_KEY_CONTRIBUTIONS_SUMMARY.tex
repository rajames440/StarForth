\section*{Pre-Submission Key Contributions Summary (Superseded)}

This pre-submission summary enumerated four formal theorems to be established
by the physics engine validation paper: (1)~algorithmic determinism, expressed
as 0\% coefficient of variation for the cache-enabled configuration;
(2)~adaptive convergence, expressed as a \mbox{$-25.4\%$} execution-time
improvement under full-physics configuration; (3)~environmental robustness,
decomposing variance into 0\% internal and 70\% OS-scheduling sources; and
(4)~predictable performance, expressed as $7.80 \pm 5.04$~ms at the 95\%
credible interval. These findings were subsequently published in the
\textsc{ssrn} paper. This summary is superseded by that publication.
